Dilex Limited

Dilex is an indigenous company with vast experience in oil spill clean up and remediation activities. We pride ourselves on being skilled and experienced in oil spill control and soil remediation services, pipeline cleaning, facility painting and platform repairs.

We are the sole representatives and long term partners of various international providers of oil spill control equipment and bionutrient products. We are committed to innovation and excellence, and that, so far, has produced enviable results.

"At Dilex Limited, we never rest on our laurels, we are driven by a desire to sustain perfect client satisfaction".

We have developed, over the past two decades, unique and flexible project management systems, which ensure that a positive outcome is achieved every time, regardless of the size or nature of the project.

Our Mission

Driven by integrity, passion for humanity and love for the environment, our mission is to bring world-class solutions and quality service to the oil and gas sector in Nigeria.
