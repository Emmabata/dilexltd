Company Overview

Dilex Limited Overview

Dilex Limited is an indigenous company with vast experience in oil spill clean up and remediation activities. We pride ourselves on being skilled and experienced in oil spill control and soil remediation services, pipeline cleaning, facility painting and platform repairs.
We also offer mechanical and electrical support services.
We are the sole representatives and long term partners of various international providers of oil spill control equipment and bionutrient products. We are committed to innovation and excellence, and that, so far, has produced enviable results.
At Dilex Limited, we never rest on our laurels, we are driven by a desire to sustain perfect client satisfaction. We have developed, over the past two decades, unique and flexible project management systems, which ensure that a positive outcome is achieved every time, regardless of the size or nature of the project.
Our values, a clear path to THE FUTURE
At the centre of our culture at DILEX LIMITED are these three principles: stewardship, excellence and progressive patriotism.
Our sense of stewardship stirs us to work earnestly towards ensuring that the earth in our care, is not set-back by our collective economic progress. And so, bearing in mind the need for sustainability, and in our bid to secure a future where earth is still habitable, we help our clients who draw from earth, give back to keeping it healthy.
Our intense passion for excellence inspires us to develop new, tailored and innovative ways to serve our clients, while also incorporating global best practices and up-to-date innovative solutions.
Our corporate patriotism plants in our hearts an ever increasing desire for the progress of our nation, especially her youth population. Hence, DILEX LIMITED, is always seeking ways to create jobs, inspire local innovation and lead conversations that keep us ever conscious of the potential hidden in every Nigerian.
We believe the path to the future is what we are already on, and that in upholding our noble values, the path ahead shines brighter. Will you join us?
